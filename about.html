About Al-Burhan Industrial Drives

Four Decades of Excellence in Power Transmission Equipment

Al-Burhan Industrial Drives is a trusted name in the power transmission equipment industry, built on over four decades of technical expertise and dedication to quality. Established and led by Mr. Zohair, a seasoned professional with 40 years of experience, the business operates from Nagdevi Street, Mumbai, one of the city's oldest and most respected industrial supply hubs.

We specialize in a comprehensive range of transmission equipment including V-belt pulleys, flat-belt pulleys, taper lock pulleys, variable speed pulleys, star couplings, flexible couplings, tyre couplings, chain couplings, nylon gear couplings, encoder couplings, gear couplings, universal joints, spur gears, bevel gears, worm gears, racks & pinions, chain sprockets and roller chains.

We proudly supply products from premium brands like LoveJoy, Fenner, Eagle, Diamond, and Renold.

Our mission is simple — deliver the right product, at the right time, with honesty and expert guidance. For over 40 years, we have earned the trust of factories, engineers, workshops, and resellers who rely on us for durable parts and practical solutions.
